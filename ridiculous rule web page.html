The Most Ridiculous Rule

I ended up quitting this job...

In the end, I quit this job because my boss had all these ridiculous expectations and he just sat in his office all day. (mind you, I was a hostess so it's not a strenuous job in the first place) His wife was so boujee and annoying and I quit after only 5 months!
